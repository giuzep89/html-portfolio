About Me

I used to be into videogames as a kid. That, in turn, got me incredibly interested in computers and technology in general, so I started tweaking my pc, add new peripherals, fixing my friends' computers, and so on.

During the following years I dedicated myself to music and ended up moving to the Netherlands to pursue this passion further with a Bachelor in Jazz saxophone at the Prins Claus Conservatorium of Groningen.

Much as I loved music, I realized that I didn't relish the life of the freelance musician, and therefore decided to explore other fields. After a few years working as a janitor, I started dedicating myself to my other great love, that is languages, working as a translator and customer service representative for about 2 years

After realizing I wanted to dedicate myself to something more challenging and creative, I decided to turn back to my early interest in technology by exploring the world of web development and coding in general. This is proving amusing and engaging in equal measure, and I can't wait to see what the future holds for me.
