My top 3 movies of all time

The Matrix

Hard not to include this one. The reason being that my brother and I really grew up with it. Spectacular, shocking, revolutionary, all those and more. I'm excited to see it now just as I was back in the day.

Independence Day

Why this movie, you may ask? Well, it's just fun. And a whole lot of it too. Everything is so exaggerated, from the visuals, the language, the events. But that's also the movie's strongest aspect: it aims really high. The sky's not the limit, at all.

Arrival

Although technically this movie involves aliens too, it couldn't be farther from the previous. Aliens are stand tall (quite literally) on the screen, but the movie is extraordinarily human-centric, with language being that which unites us and divides us at the same time. A true masterpiece.
